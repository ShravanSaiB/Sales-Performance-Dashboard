## Insights 

- Region West has Highest Sales with ₹ 3,50,75,942. 
- Product Laptop has Highest Sales with ₹ 3,01,79,810. 
- In 2023, month July Has Highest Sales with ₹ 1,54,64,763. 
- In 2024, month January has Low Sales with ₹ 3,06,946. 

## Recommendations
 
- Need to check the Cause of July month sales reasons and bring it into action for every month for        
  boosting sales. 
- Need to attract Customers by allowing more Festival offers, Discounts, Gifts. 
- Products with less Demand can be paired with high demanded Products with good Discounts for          
  Stock clearance.
